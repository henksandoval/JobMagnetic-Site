What is an Early Adopter View?

It is a preliminary version of a product or feature designed for users who want to try new functionalities before their official release. These users are key to providing feedback and helping us improve.

Key Features

  • Limited access to a select group of users.
  • Experimental and developing interface.
  • Active feedback to improve the product.
  • Exploratory purpose to validate ideas.

How can you help?

As an Early Adopter, you will have the opportunity to test our developing features and provide your valuable feedback. Your participation is essential to help us build a better product.
